ABOUT THE PATRAS LEAGUE

Key features

Team

You can create your own team on the desired sport. You can have one brand name but different members for each different sport. At the start of the season you can enter the local league and reach the top. Please read more about team formation and entrance in our community here.

Facilities

You can rent for your team practices one of our facilities. Please check our monthly payment offers in order to give you access. We have a variety of stadiums for each sport that we support.

Winnings

Each Season we give the winning team in the score board exclusive prices.After that this team will represent us in a pan-local championship with other teams of Achaia in order to go for the Pan-Hellenic.

Sports

We provide Leagues in the field of Soccer, Basketball, Volleyball, Tennis, Handball etc. You can check the full list of sports here. Each season there is need to be filled the least amount of teams/participants in order the league to start.
